About Karen

Passion Meets Expertise

With over 20 years in the hair industry, I've dedicated my career to making every bride feel absolutely radiant on their special day. As a senior stylist specializing in bridal hair, color correction, and extension work, I bring both technical expertise and artistic vision to every appointment.

Based in New Ross, Co. Wexford, I serve brides throughout the Southeast of Ireland, offering both salon services and on-location styling for your wedding day. My approach combines the latest techniques with timeless elegance, ensuring your bridal look is uniquely yours.
